Welcome to ICSM Group Meeting

ICSM group meeting starts from Oct 18 2012, which aims to create a good communication atomosphere among PICB students. ICSM stands for Image processing, Computer vision, Signal processing and Machine learning, as the content of the group meeting.

Schedule and Content

ICSM group meeting is held twice every week. It starts from 7:30pm~9:00pm on Monday and from 3:30pm~5:00pm on Friday. On Monday we talk about a specified book by turns. There will be one person arranged to introduce one chapter of the book for 1.5 hours. On Friday everyone is free to talk about anything that is interesting for about 15 minutes or more.

Members and Join us

Currently there are 8 regular group members who are from differents contries and different groups. To make the group much bigger and much more important, we encourage you, who has the same interest, to join us. You will learn lots of useful knowledge, get practiced and have fun. It is better that you can contribute talks regularly with us.

Please contact picbicsm@gmail.com, if you want to join us or have questions.
